Fair Trade for Auburn University Graduate Students

Graduate students at Auburn University currently endure:

  1. Increased registration fees;
  2. Increased health insurance fees;
  3. Proration fees charged to students who have tuition waived or paid for;
  4. Funding cuts;
  5. Removal of facility priviledges.

Do you feel unfair to pay over 1.3K each semester for all kinds of fees? Do you think it doesn't make sense to pay extra for gym access?
